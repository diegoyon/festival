Featured DJ's

Afrojack

#6 DJ according to DJ Magazine

Afrojack is a Dutch DJ, music producer and remixer from Spijkenisse. In 2007, he founded the record label Wall Recordings; his debut album Forget the World was released in 2014. He is also the CEO of LDH Europe.

Armin Van Buuren

#3 DJ according to DJ Magazine

His five-time number one position in the critically acclaimed DJ Mag Top 100 DJs Poll has been the result of his loyalty to fans, his creativity in the studio, the perseverance with which he hosts his weekly radio show ‘A State Of Trance’.

DJ Snake

#33 DJ according to DJ Magazine

DJ Snake, is a French record producer, DJ and songwriter, first achieving international recognition in 2013 by releasing an instrumentation-orientated single called "Turn Down for What" (featuring Lil Jon).

Carl Cox

#27 DJ according to DJ Magazine

Cox has won and been nominated for numerous awards. He has performed at numerous clubs and electronic music or dance events worldwide and has served as a monthly DJ for BBC Radio 1's Essential Mix.

David Guetta

#1 DJ according to DJ Magazine

David Guetta is a French DJ and music producer. He has over 50 million record sales globally, with more than 10 billion streams.

Tiesto

#15 DJ according to DJ Magazine

Tiesto is a Dutch DJ and music producer from Breda. He was voted "the Greatest DJ of All Time" by Mix magazine in a 2010/2011 poll amongst fans. In 2013, he was voted by DJ Mag readers as the "best DJ of the last 20 years".
